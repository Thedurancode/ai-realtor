### Document Management Workflow:

**Contract to Close:**
1. **Offer Received**
   - Review offer terms
   - Create counter-offer if needed
   - Negotiate terms
   - Accept offer
   - Send to attorney for review

2. **Attorney Review (1-3 days)**
   - Attorney reviews contract
   - Requests changes/addendums
   - Clears contingencies

3. **Inspection Period (7-14 days)**
   - Schedule home inspection
   - Coordinate access
   - Receive inspection report
   - Negotiate repairs or credits
   - Repair addendum or waiver

4. **Mortgage Application (5-10 days)**
   - Buyer applies for mortgage
   - Submit financial documents
   - Lender orders appraisal
   - Loan commitment issued

5. **Appraisal (7-14 days)**
   - Schedule appraisal
   - Provide comps to appraiser
   - Receive appraisal report
   - Address low appraisal if needed

6. **Title Search (7-14 days)**
   - Title company runs search
   - Clear any liens/encumbrances
   - Prepare title commitment

7. **Final Walkthrough (3-5 days before closing)**
   - Schedule walkthrough
   - Verify property condition
   - Confirm repairs completed
   - Check utilities transferred

8. **Closing**
   - Schedule closing appointment
   - Review closing disclosure (3 days prior)
   - Final utility readings
   - Transfer funds
   - Sign documents
   - Record deed
   - Hand over keys
